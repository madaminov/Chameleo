Communication Styles

Communication styles various in different countries so to communicate effectively learn other's country style

China

People are direct. They say exactly what they mean, even if it might sound a bit blunt. For example, if they don’t like something, they might say, “I don’t think this is a good idea.”

Example

If someone’s work needs improvement, they might say, “This isn’t good enough. Please redo it.” In other cultures people use more indirect language to avoid offending others, prioritize relationships, and maintain harmony.

USA

People are more polite. They don’t always say exactly what they mean, and they try not to hurt anyone’s feelings. They might say something like, “Maybe we can try something else” instead of saying directly that something is wrong

Example

Instead of directly pointing out a problem, they might say, “This is good, but maybe we could consider a few changes?”
